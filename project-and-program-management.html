Case Study
ACT Health IPMO

ACT Health is embarking on a significant capital investment program with a combined value of $1.2 billion of acute and community health projects. In parallel, ACT Health was seeking to implement a $26 million recurrent services program, which includes a diverse range of service improvement projects focused on the achievement of business operational efficiency outcomes.

SAFM was engaged to establish an enduring Integrated Program Management Office (IPMO) for the effective implementation and delivery of the capital and recurrent programs.

The IPMO included:

  • a consolidated reporting mechanism for controlled and informed investment decisions;
  • design of templates, business systems and documentation for consistency in the discipline of project management, risk management, and budgeting;
  • real-time reporting on program status, delays and control strategies; and
  • a platform for stakeholder engagement and communications, co-ordinated across the portfolio.
