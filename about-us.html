Meet the Directors
Mick Serena

A results-oriented Managing Director, adept at creating strong working relationships to deliver innovative client solutions designed to achieve strategic objectives. Mick has extensive experience across the higher education, government and private sectors in strategic and operational planning, project delivery, asset and facilities management, business improvement as well as organisational and cultural change.

Mick’s leadership and client-centric approach has enabled the company to establish a strong record of delivering excellent and innovative outcomes which provide value for money and are of enduring benefit to clients. Our clients include a number of G08 Universities including UQ, Monash, the University of Adelaide and ANU, a range of other Australian and New Zealand universities such as Murdoch University, University of Canberra, JCU, the University of Auckland and University of Otago. Mick has also worked with organisations spanning all levels of Government; Commonwealth, State, Territory and Councils.

Vanessa Brady

A successful leader with a demonstrated capability of developing collaborative partnerships across business to deliver outcomes, Vanessa has over 19 years commercial property experience in senior executive client-side project management, program management and strategic procurement roles.

Vanessa has successfully managed cumulatively over $3.5 billion of capital works projects, which range from secure government facilities; scientific and research facilities, specialist health facilities, bio-containment facilities, commercial office and mixed-use developments. This diverse construction experience includes greenfield and brownfield construction projects; heritage and major refurbishment projects.

Vanessa has a proven track record in the planning and delivery of significant and complex major capital works projects, programs and procurements, ranging in value from $15 million to $1.8 billion. Vanessa’s strong focus on client commercial outcomes, her high degree of business acumen and innovative procurement methodologies has resulted in the successful delivery of sustainable client solutions.

Lisa Brown

Lisa is a results-orientated senior manager with extensive experience in strategic workplace services, accommodation management, procurement and contract management, project and program management and organisational performance improvement across the government, tertiary education, health, and not for profit sectors. She has a Master of Public Policy and Management, coupled with a proven track record of accomplishment in space planning and management, asset and facilities planning, government service delivery, and client and stakeholder management.

In her current role as a Director, Lisa designs practical, innovative solutions to client challenges through developing strategies including benchmarking best practice to improve organisational performance in the areas of asset and facilities planning of built assets, space optimisation, and the establishment of organisational capability and capacity.  Lisa is credited with developing a suite of new products and services to address a range of complex problems, providing clients with value for money and enduring benefits.

Wayne Ford

Wayne is an enthusiastic and resourceful professional with a wealth of experience in optimising infrastructure, facilities, construction, property and investment management across diverse sectors through outstanding senior executive level management, client-side project management, program management and leadership skills.

Wayne has worked with some of the country’s largest infrastructure groups – having worked at Sydney Water and largest property groups, trusts, funds, owners and occupiers across the office, industrial/business park, retail and higher education sectors - Go8 and international universities.
