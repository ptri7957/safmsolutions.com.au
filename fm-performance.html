Case Study
Amana Living Building Condition Reports

Amana Living is one of WA's largest not-for-profit providers of care and services for older people. SAFM Solutions were engaged to provide Amana with Building Condition Reports for 29 of its facilities including both maintenance and capital projected expenditure, a condition rating for each element and a summary of each facility.

The reports were prepared based on physical condition assessments of each facility. An overall portfolio summary repport, consisting of a summary of each facility, an overall summary for the portfolio of facilities, a 10-year projected maintenance and capital expenditure budget, and facility ranking table was also provided. This greatly improved Amanas understanding of it's asset portfolio and improved their asset intelligence.
